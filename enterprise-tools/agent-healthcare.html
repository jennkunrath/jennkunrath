Capacity model: staff × loaded salary × automatable-work% × automation-efficiency% + annual overhead reduction (McKinsey GenAI: 60–70% of healthcare activities automatable; concentrated in clinical documentation, prior auth, coding, scheduling, denials work, patient communication). Quality & risk model: weekly event volume × weeks × defect-rate × defect-reduction × cost-per-defect (covers claim rework, RAC takebacks, denied-appeal work, audit-finding remediation, care-gap closure, HIPAA breach exposure; deterministic + GenAI hybrid with EHR grounding and payer-policy retrieval reduces defects 65–80%).
